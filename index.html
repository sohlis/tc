About Triple Clear

Inside the Technology

Triple Clear Technology

Triple Clear Powder (TCP) is a filtration media based on nanotechnology. It starts with coconut shells which are converted to grains of highly porous carbon base material. Then, using a proprietary method based on technologies created for the semiconductor industry, they are plated with high purity elements.

The result? One gram of Triple Clear Powder has an internal adsorptive surface area equivalent to a Soccer field!

This is more than 8x the normal absorptive qualities of activated carbon. It’s like a “super sponge.” Plus, unlike normal granular activated carbon, our patented process leaves every grain with both bactericidal and bacteriostatic properties. This means it doesn’t just kill bacteria, it keeps killing bacteria for days after treatment, while leaving no chemicals or ions in the water. We kill bacteria using just oxygen!

Using no electricity or chemicals, Triple Clear: Filters and suppresses heavy metals, hydrocarbons, pesticides, nitrates, micro-pollutants, oxidation byproducts, detergents, other harmful chemical products, coloring, tastes and odors; Oxidizes toxins and bacteria on contact, including bacteria that are resistant to chlorine, such as Legionella Pneumophila and, amazingly, because of its residual properties, it preserves water naturally and protects for an extended time period against recontamination It’s not only the most effective process available, it’s completely GREEN!

The Science

Triple Clear Powder is more than just Granulated Active Carbon (GAC)!

The grafting of very high purity metals onto the internal and external micro-pore surfaces of the active carbon is done through a proprietary process derived upon technologies created for the semiconductor industry. Our Chief Science Officer developed the hardware systems and processes in-house using ground-breaking techniques from the nano-scale semiconductor industry.

The deposits obtained on the GAC is said to be "in thin layers" with controlled thickness of a few dozens angstroms. The catalytic elements are covalently bonded (i.e., strong atomic bonds) to the virgin active carbon, and due to the purity of the materials deposited provide stability to the metallic ions and will not induce metal oxidation, which assures an excellent reactivity in water.

The net result is that Triple Clear Powder combines the well-known adsorptive qualities of granulated activated carbon with the immediate bactericidal and bacteriostatic qualities.

Without the use of electricity or a photo source (example ~ UV light), the natural catalytic reaction created by water flowing through the Triple Clear Powder purifies water to a certifiable potable state**, with the added benefits of oxidizing toxins and bacteria on contact (including bacteria that are resistant to chlorine, such as Legionella Pneumophila) and preserves water naturally for an extended period of time against recontamination.
